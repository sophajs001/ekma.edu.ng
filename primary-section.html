Discovering Early Learning Pathways

From Creche care to Primary excellence

Early childhood education is the foundation of lifelong learning. At EKMA, our Creche, Nursery, and Primary programs nurture curiosity, creativity, and confidence from the very start. With caring teachers and a safe environment, children develop essential skills in literacy, numeracy, and social interaction. We emphasize play-based learning alongside structured academics, ensuring every child grows with joy and purpose. Choosing EKMA means giving your child the best start to thrive in school and beyond.

Creche

At the creche level, children begin their learning journey through guided play, communication development, and structured interaction.

Nursery

Nursery classes introduce foundational literacy, numeracy, and creativity through Montessori learning methods and engaging activities.

Primary School

Pupils develop strong academic foundations in Mathematics, English, Science, and Social Studies while building discipline and leadership skills.

  • Basic 1–3: Foundational literacy, numeracy, social skills, and creativity.
  • Basic 4–6: Advanced academics, critical thinking, and leadership skills.

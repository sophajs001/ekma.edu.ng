Our Journey Through Time

From humble beginnings to a beacon of excellence

Excellent Kiddies Montessori Academy was founded to provide high-quality education and holistic development for children. Over the years, the school has nurtured generations of learners by focusing on intellectual growth, character development, creativity, and social responsibility.

2005 – Foundation

Commencement & Early Years

Excellent Kiddies Montessori Academy commenced academic activities on Monday, 19th September 2005 with four pupils, two teachers, and two non-teaching staff.

The school started with Playgroup, Nursery, and Primary 1 & 2. From inception, the focus was on quality teaching, relevant academic activities, and good moral development, which led to rapid growth in enrollment.

2006 – 2015

Growth & Recognition

Parents and guardians within Bwari and environs were delighted with the excellent performances of their children in internal and external exams, as well as competitions.

During this period, EKMA won multiple competitions including Spelling Bee (FCT first position 8 times, National second position), JETS (National first position twice), Chess (FCT first position twice), and Regional Essay Competitions where pupils even acted as a bank manager for a day.

2016 – Present

Expansion & Innovation

Today, EKMA has grown to include Nursery, Primary, Junior, and Senior Secondary schools with about 700 pupils and students, supported by around 100 teaching and non-teaching staff.

The school consistently records excellent performance in WAEC, NECO, and BECE. Alumni are now engineers, bankers, teachers, accountants, medical practitioners, IT professionals, and more.

EKMA also trains pupils and students in coding, programming, and entrepreneurial skills such as barbing, cosmetology, shoemaking, tailoring, photography, electrical work, catering, computer repairs, and GSM maintenance.

Our Mission

Welcome to Excellent Kiddies Montessori Academy, where we believe that education is the foundation for a brighter future. Our mission is to create an inclusive and inspiring environment where every child’s unique talents are nurtured, and their potential is realized. Through a balanced approach to academics, personal development, and creative expression, we empower students to become confident, compassionate leaders who are ready to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

We are committed to excellence in education, fostering a culture of curiosity, resilience, and integrity. Our experienced faculty, innovative programs, and vibrant community all work together to provide each student with the tools they need to succeed both in and out of the classroom.

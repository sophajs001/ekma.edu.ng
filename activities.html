Activities at EKMA

From academics to arts, sports, clubs, and community service — discover how EKMA nurtures the whole child

Education is more than classroom learning. At Excellent Kiddies Montessori Academy, we combine academics with extracurricular activities — sports, arts, clubs, vocational skills, and community service — to nurture the whole child.

This holistic approach ensures that learners grow intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically. By engaging in diverse activities, children develop creativity, leadership, resilience, and practical life skills. Education cuts across many aspects, and that is why we emphasize vocational training, arts, sports, and personal development alongside rigorous academics.

At EKMA, we believe in whole-person formation: preparing students not only for exams, but for life. Choosing EKMA means choosing a balanced education that empowers your child to thrive in every sphere.

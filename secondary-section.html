Your Secondary School Journey

From Junior Secondary foundations to WAEC success

Secondary school is a transformative stage in education, shaping students both academically and socially. At EKMA, learners gain not only knowledge but also vital life skills that prepare them for higher education and beyond. Our structured programs build confidence and responsibility, ensuring every child grows into a well‑rounded individual. With dedicated teachers and a nurturing environment, EKMA provides personalized guidance that helps students excel in WAEC and future pursuits. We emphasize character development alongside academics, giving learners the tools to thrive in society. Choosing EKMA means laying a strong foundation for lifelong success.

Explore the Stages

Each stage is essential in shaping your future

Junior Secondary

The first three years of secondary school. Focus on Mathematics, English, and Science. Builds literacy and numeracy skills. Introduces vocational and social studies. Prepares learners for senior classes.

Senior Secondary

The final three years of school. Students choose Science, Arts, or Commerce. Subjects deepen for career readiness. Builds discipline and responsibility. Prepares learners for external exams.

External Examinations

WAEC and NECO are key assessments. They test mastery of chosen subjects. Results determine higher education entry. Success opens doors to careers. Marks the completion of secondary school.

Curriculum Overview: Junior & Senior Secondary

Level Core Subjects Electives / Streams
Junior Secondary (JSS 1–3) English Language, Mathematics, Basic Science & Technology, Social Studies, Civic Education, Computer Studies, Physical & Health Education, Religious Studies (CRS / IRS). Introductory Technology, Creative Arts, Business Studies, Agricultural Science, French.
Senior Secondary – Science Stream English Language, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Further Mathematics, Computer Science, Religious Studies (CRS / IRS). Agricultural Science, Geography, Technical Drawing, Health Science.
Senior Secondary – Arts & Commercial Stream English Language, Mathematics, Literature in English, Government, Economics, CRS / IRS, Civic Education. Commerce, Accounting, Geography, Fine Arts, History.

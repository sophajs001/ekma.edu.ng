Celebrating Our Achievements

From academic excellence to community impact

At EKMA, achievements are more than milestones, they are stories of dedication, creativity, and resilience. Our students shine in academics, sports, arts, and service, proving that excellence comes in many forms. Each success reflects not only talent but also the values of hard work and community spirit. From WAEC distinctions to national competitions, from charity drives to leadership summits, EKMA’s achievements inspire pride and set a standard for future generations. We celebrate these accomplishments as a testament to our mission: nurturing well‑rounded individuals ready to thrive in society.
